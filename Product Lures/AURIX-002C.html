Product Description

The AURIX-002C "Ember Glow Strike" features a captivating golden-amber to red gradient on the obverse side, paired with a mirror-chrome holographic reverse that flashes brilliantly underwater. This dynamic combination replicates the natural movement and shimmer of baitfish—perfect for enticing mid-depth and offshore predators.

The reverse side incorporates dual Red and Green glow zones emit a pulsating light under low-light conditions, increasing visibility and triggering reaction strikes from afar. Designed with a balanced aerodynamic profile, the jig maintains stability during long-distance drops and strong currents.

Ideal For

Highly effective in both coastal and offshore jigging, targeting species such as kingfish, dorado, and grouper in low-light or deep-water environments.
