DRAK #C001: Story Line

The **DRAK #C001** is a meticulously crafted jig designed for anglers who demand performance and resilience. Its sleek, aerodynamic profile allows for a rapid descent and a darting action that perfectly mimics an injured baitfish. Built from a robust **lead alloy** and sealed with a protective **polyurethane coating**, it's engineered to withstand the harshest conditions without compromising its effectiveness.
