About Us

Day1Craft Philippines Inc. (D1CPI) is one of the fastest growing manufacturing company engaged in the production of finest, hand-crafted fishing lures. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Japan-based parent company, MARUYONE CO., LTD. Established in 2014, D1CPI, as the strongest production arm, continuously innovates in order to produce product that would give the best quality fishing experience to all of its customers.

Vision

D1CPI continues to envisions becoming a globally recognized top producer of quality fishing lures.

Mission

D1CPI believes that through its people - the company's most competitive advantage, it can deliver premiere quality product through the effective execution of operating principles of Safety mind-set, Quality Product, People and Processes, Timely Delivery, Cost Effectiveness and Continued Improvement (Kaizen).
